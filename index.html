Meet Robert

Robert is one of the co-founders of a company called Kanpla. It is an app, which helps for the schools to reduce food waste, create a responsible production and ensure energy for the whole school day with a healthy meal.

Kanpla has been nominated for Sustainable Tech Startup of the Year 2020. They are focus on food waste and responsible production and they constantly try to influence the canteen in a more sustainable, organic and green direction.

Robert is a former student of GROW. He just finished his studies at Innovation and Entrepreneurship at Business Academy Aarhus. His story can be an inspiration for all the students at the Academy, how they can benefit from joining the GROW and start their own company.
